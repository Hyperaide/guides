## Quick Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in Hyperaide
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect TickTick">
    Click **TickTick** and authorize access
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Managing">
    Create and manage tasks via your assistant!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

```text
Create: "Add a TickTick task to review quarterly goals"
View: "Show my TickTick tasks for today"
Complete: "Mark the goals review task as done"
Update: "Move the task to next Monday"
Habits: "Did I complete my reading habit today?"
```

<Note>
  TickTick is perfect if you want powerful task management features (habits, Pomodoro, calendars) with natural language control.
</Note>
