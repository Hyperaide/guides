## Setting Up Todoist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in the Hyperaide web app
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Todoist">
    Find and click the **Todoist** integration card
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Click **Connect** and log in to your Todoist account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Authorize Hyperaide to read and manage your Todoist tasks
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Using">
    Your assistant can now access your Todoist tasks!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Hyperaide requests read and write access to your Todoist tasks. We never delete tasks without your explicit instruction.
</Info>

## What Your Assistant Can Do

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Create Tasks">
    Add tasks to Todoist using natural language:
    
    ```text
    "Create a Todoist task to review the budget by Friday"
    "Add to my Todoist: Call the dentist tomorrow at 2pm"
    "New Todoist task in the Work project: Finish presentation"
    "Add a high priority task to buy groceries"
    ```
    
    Your assistant automatically:
    - Parses dates ("tomorrow", "Friday", "in 3 days")
    - Detects projects and labels
    - Sets priority levels
    - Adds to the correct project
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="View Tasks">
    Query your Todoist tasks naturally:
    
    ```text
    "Show me all my Todoist tasks"
    "What Todoist tasks are due today?"
    "List my high priority Todoist tasks"
    "Show tasks in my Work project"
    "What's overdue in Todoist?"
    ```
    
    Your assistant displays tasks with:
    - Task name and description
    - Due dates and times
    - Priority levels
    - Project and labels
    - Completion status
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Complete Tasks">
    Mark tasks as complete:
    
    ```text
    "Complete the Todoist task about the budget review"
    "Mark the first three Todoist tasks as done"
    "I finished calling the dentist"
    "Complete all Todoist tasks due today"
    ```
    
    Your assistant finds the right task and marks it complete in Todoist.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Update Tasks">
    Modify existing tasks:
    
    ```text
    "Move the budget review task to next Monday"
    "Change the priority of the presentation task to high"
    "Update the dentist task description to include insurance card"
    "Move the task to the Personal project"
    ```
    
    Your assistant updates tasks in Todoist immediately.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Manage Projects">
    Work with Todoist projects:
    
    ```text
    "What projects do I have in Todoist?"
    "Show all tasks in the Marketing project"
    "How many tasks are in my Work project?"
    "List tasks from the Q1 Goals project"
    ```
    
    Your assistant understands your project structure.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Permissions & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What Hyperaide Can Access" icon="eye">
    - Read all your Todoist tasks
    - View projects and labels
    - See task metadata (dates, priorities, etc.)
    - Access task descriptions and comments
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What Hyperaide Can Do" icon="pen">
    - Create new tasks in any project
    - Update task details (dates, priorities, descriptions)
    - Mark tasks as complete
    - Move tasks between projects
    
    Only when you explicitly instruct it to do so.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What Hyperaide Cannot Do" icon="shield-xmark">
    - Delete tasks (unless you explicitly request it)
    - Access your Todoist account settings
    - Share your tasks with anyone else
    - Modify tasks without your instruction
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  Hyperaide has full access to your Todoist account. Only connect if you trust Hyperaide to manage your tasks.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Tasks Not Syncing" icon="arrows-rotate">
    **Possible solutions:**
    1. Check that the integration is connected in Settings
    2. Wait 30-60 seconds for sync to complete
    3. Try disconnecting and reconnecting Todoist
    4. Verify the task exists in Todoist
    5. Check for any Todoist service outages
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't Find Task" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Possible solutions:**
    1. Try searching with different keywords
    2. Check if the task is in a different project
    3. Verify the task isn't completed or deleted
    4. Ask "Show all my Todoist tasks" to see everything
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Authorization Expired" icon="key">
    **Solution:**
    Todoist tokens can expire for security:
    
    1. Go to Settings → Integrations
    2. Find Todoist and click **Reconnect**
    3. Re-authorize access
    4. Your integration resumes working
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong Project Assignment" icon="folder-xmark">
    **Solutions:**
    1. Be more explicit: "Add to the Work project"
    2. Use exact project names from Todoist
    3. Your assistant will ask if ambiguous
    4. You can move tasks after creation
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Disconnecting Todoist

To disconnect Todoist from Hyperaide:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Integrations**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find Todoist">
    Locate your Todoist integration
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Disconnect">
    Click **Disconnect** and confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Revoke Access (Optional)">
    Visit Todoist settings to fully revoke Hyperaide's access
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Disconnecting doesn't delete your Todoist tasks—it only removes Hyperaide's ability to access them. Your tasks remain safe in Todoist.
</Info>

<Note>
  Todoist + Hyperaide is perfect if you're already a Todoist user but want natural language control and cross-platform automation. Connect it and you'll never need to open Todoist manually again!
</Note>
