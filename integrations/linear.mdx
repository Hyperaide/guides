## Setting Up Linear

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Integrations">
    Navigate to **Settings â†’ Integrations**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Linear">
    Click the **Linear** integration card
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Sign in and authorize Hyperaide to access your Linear workspace
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Teams">
    Select which Linear teams to sync
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Managing">
    Your assistant can now interact with Linear!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart Features

- **Automatic team detection**: Mentions project names to assign correct team
- **Status mapping**: Understands "todo", "in progress", "done" etc.
- **Priority intelligence**: Maps "urgent", "high", "normal", "low"
- **Issue linking**: Connect Linear issues to Hyperaide tasks
