Slack integration will bring your Hyperaide assistant directly into your team's workspace, enabling seamless collaboration and personal productivity within your professional environment.

## Planned Features

## How It Will Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install App">
    Install Hyperaide bot to your Slack workspace
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize">
    Connect your Hyperaide account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="DM Your Assistant">
    Send direct messages for personal tasks
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use in Channels">
    @mention in channels for team visibility
  </Step>
</Steps>
