## Setting Up Notion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Settings â†’ Integrations** in Hyperaide
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Notion">
    Click the **Notion** integration card
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Sign in to Notion and grant Hyperaide access
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Pages">
    Select which pages/databases to sync
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Using">
    Your assistant can now work with Notion!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart Features

- **Fuzzy page search**: Finds pages even with partial names
- **Database recognition**: Understands your Notion database structures
- **Property mapping**: Automatically maps to the right fields
- **Content extraction**: Pulls relevant information from long pages
